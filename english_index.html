What is DEXIM?

DEXIM is a web application for market research of exports and imports in Peru.

Using DEXIM you can see the market share of your company, study the costs of your competitors, analyze the evolution of the market size etc.

Our source of information are the customs records of the government office SUNAT. Records that we process to show you useful insights for your business.
